Validation Hub

While working at Thrillworks, I built a responsive mirco-site that served as a QA tool for the testing of various promotions on a client website. The site quickly grew into a hub, including quick-links to other frequently used client technologies and documents.

This hub gave everyone easier access to the tools they needed, and improved the QA process, giving greater visibility across all localized regions and their staging/production environments.
